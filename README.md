# 📊 Sales Data Analysis & Business Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data to uncover key business insights and support decision-making. The goal is to identify patterns, improve profitability, and highlight growth opportunities.

---

## ❓ Business Questions

This analysis answers important business questions:

* Which regions generate the highest profit?
* Which products are the most and least profitable?
* How do sales trends change over time?
* What factors affect profit margins?

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Pivot Tables
* Dashboard Design

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard includes:

* 💰 Total Sales
* 📦 Total Orders
* 📊 Total Profit
* 📉 Profit Margin
* 🌍 Sales by Region
* 📅 Sales Trends

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Some regions achieve higher profitability despite lower sales volume
* Certain products generate high revenue but low profit margins
* Sales trends show fluctuations across time, indicating potential seasonality
* Opportunities exist to improve pricing and reduce costs
